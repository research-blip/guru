About Kriya Yoga

Kriya Yoga, as practised and taught in this lineage, is a profound yet simple spiritual science that purifies the blood and recharges it with life-giving oxygen. This oxygen is transmuted into prana, the subtle life energy, which awakens and revitalises the brain and spinal centres — guiding the practitioner toward self-realisation. It transcends faith and belief.

Swamiji Background

Swami Vimuktananda is a realised Kriya Yogi and direct disciple of Paramhans Yogananda. He received his first diksha (spiritual initiation) from Yoganandaji at the tender age of 11, marking the beginning of a lifelong journey in the sacred science of Kriya Yoga. Swami Vimuktananda later deepened his spiritual training under the revered Bhupendranath Sanyalji and his son, Kashipatinath Sanyalji — both torchbearers of the ancient Kriya Yoga lineage that began over 5,000 years ago with the immortal Mahavatar Babaji. This unbroken lineage has passed through luminaries like Lahiri Mahasaya, Yukteshwar Giriji and Paramhansa Yogananda, carrying forward the light of inner transformation.
